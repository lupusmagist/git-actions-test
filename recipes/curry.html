Basic Curry recipe.


Ingredients:

Spice Mix:

  • Powdered garlic.
  • Powdered ginger.
  • Powdered cinnimmen.
  • Powdered cumin.
  • Powdered tumuric.
  • Chilli - fresh or powdered to taste.

Other Ingredients:

  • 500-750g Meat - Chicken or beaf - cubed. (Chicken breast and/or thigh)
  • 125ml Coconut milk.
  • 15ml Viniger
  • 2x Potatoes cubed (1cm cubes)
  • Carrots or other root vegetables.
  • Cornstarch to thicken. (Optional)

Preperation:

Dry fry spices in pan. Remove from pan. Fry meat in oil/butter in pot until cooked. Remove from pot. Fry onions, carrots, peppers and potatoes in pot until onions are golden. (Slow fry) Return the meat to the pot. Add the fried spice, coconut milk and viniger and cook until meat is soft.

Optional: (If potatoes is not used or if potatoes does not thinken sauce) Mix cornstarch with cold water and add little by little to pot until desired thinkness is reached.

Serve with rice or Nan Bread
