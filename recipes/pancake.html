Basic Pancakes


Ingredients:

  • 2 Cups Milk
  • 2 Cups Flour
  • 2 Eggs
  • 80ml Sugar
  • 80ml Oil
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Preperation:

Mix eggs, milk and oil in large mixing bowl.
Add 1 cup of flour and mix until no lumps.
Add the other cup of flour and mix until no lumps.
Add sugar, baking powder and mix well.

Let mixture stand in fridge for 1 hour minimum, but longer is better.

Preheat pancake pan to medium heat and wipe with oil when hot.
Pour pancake batter using a soup laddle into pan and swirl until batter covers pan.
Flip pancake when bubbles form on the top and the bottom is light brown when lifted.
